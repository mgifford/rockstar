Emmet Brickowski

Franchise: The LEGO Movie

Emmet starts as an ordinary construction worker and becomes a symbol of creativity and teamwork. His journey proves that anyone can build something extraordinary with confidence and imagination.

Wyldstyle (Lucy)

Franchise: The LEGO Movie

Wyldstyle blends artistic flair with tactical skill. She challenges the status quo, pushes others to think bigger, and remains one of the most dynamic personalities in the LEGO universe.

LEGO Batman

Franchise: The LEGO Batman Movie

Loud, dramatic, and endlessly entertaining, LEGO Batman turns every mission into a show. Behind the swagger is a hero who learns the value of trust, friendship, and family.

Kai

Franchise: LEGO NINJAGO

Kai is a passionate fire ninja known for bold action and strong loyalty. His growth from hot-headed fighter to dependable leader is a key part of the NINJAGO story.

Lloyd Garmadon

Franchise: LEGO NINJAGO

As the green ninja, Lloyd carries major responsibility with calm determination. He often stands at the center of NINJAGO's biggest battles and moral choices.

Princess Unikitty

Franchise: The LEGO Movie

Unikitty is joyful and vibrant, but never one-dimensional. Her quick emotional range and fearless attitude make her both comedic and surprisingly powerful when things get intense.
